Biography

Angela Yam, soprano, is a Boston-based stage performer and concert soloist.

A lover of languages, Ms. Yam obtained a minor in Linguistics from UC Davis, and studied Italian opera and language at Lingua e canto, a language-intensive summer program in Sant’Angelo in Vado, Italy. She is currently pursuing a Graduate Diploma at the New England Conservatory of Music as a student of Lisa Saffer. Prior teachers include Dr. Robin Fisher and Zoila Muñoz. Ms. Yam is the Artistic Director of Causeway Chamber Soloists, an ensemble that performs vocal chamber music. She enjoys playing the piano, violin, and baritone saxophone in her spare time.

Biography

Boston-based soprano Angela Yam is a performer whose repertoire ranges from opera to oratorio to avant-garde chamber music. While finishing her Graduate Diploma degree from the New England Conservatory (2021), Angela has performed as the Coloratura Soprano in Argento's Postcard from Morocco, as the titular role in Cavalli's La Calisto, and as the soprano soloist in Haydn's Die Sch√∂pfung.

Angela recently appeared as Mary Crawford in Dove's Mansfield Park with Opera Modesto and as the soprano soloist for Bach's Magnificat and Handel's Messiah with Camerata California. An avid recitalist, she has self-produced 7 unique, hour-long recitals since 2016, and is the Artistic Director of Causeway Chamber Soloists. In her spare time, she enjoys roller-skating and playing the baritone saxophone.
